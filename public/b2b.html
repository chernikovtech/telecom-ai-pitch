GPU-as-a-Service Platform
Sell raw compute and inference capacity to enterprises, startups, and government agencies in your market. You own the GPUs (or co-invest with us), they rent tokens. This is the core infrastructure play — the AI equivalent of owning cell towers.
  • Own the inference layer
